Frequently asked questions

How fast will I hear back?
We aim to respond to messages within 1-2 business days. For lender-matching status questions, the fastest path is usually to contact the lender directly after the match is completed.
Is MMM a lender?
No. MMM is a comparison and matching service. We connect borrowers with lenders (our primary partner is Lendmate Capital) but we don’t originate loans or make lending decisions ourselves.
Can I request deletion of my data?
Yes. Send a request through this form (or email contact@mymoneymarketplace.com) specifying “deletion request” and we’ll process it per our Privacy Policy. Some information may be retained as required by law.
How does MMM make money?
We earn compensation from partner lenders when users we refer complete an application. See our Disclosures page for details.
